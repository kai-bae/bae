CSR

Committed to Enriching Society

At BAE, we believe that business success should go hand in hand with societal well-being. As our company grows, so should the communities around us. We align our operations with society’s needs, ensuring our impact extends beyond profits to sustainable development.

Our commitment to sustainability includes environmental efforts like tree planting and planned solar power installations. But it goes further—we embrace ethical business practices, responsible manufacturing, and initiatives that foster shared prosperity. By integrating social responsibility into our operations, we strive to create lasting value for both our company and society.

Tree Planting

BAE values environmental sustainability and community well-being. Our team, including the MD and Deputy MD, planted tree seedlings in the vast field of Factory 2 in Chachoengsao province. Over time, these trees have grown strong, transforming the once-barren land into a thriving green space. As many of our employees call this province home, we see this not just as environmental action but as nurturing the community itself—providing not only livelihoods but also a healthier, greener place to live for future generations.
