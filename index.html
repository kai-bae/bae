About Us

Brother Auto Parts & Engineering (BAE) is a leading first-tier manufacturer of stamping parts, proudly serving the Thai automotive industry for over 30 years.

With decades of expertise, we have developed technology proficiency, experience, and knowledge that consistently meet the high standards set by automotive makers. Our focus areas include quality control, cost control, and project management, ensuring we deliver exactly what our clients expect.
